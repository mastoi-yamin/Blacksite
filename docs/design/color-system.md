# Color System

## Theme

Dark mode only.

No light theme is planned.

---

## Color Philosophy

Colors should communicate confidence, clarity, and professionalism.

Use color intentionally.

Avoid rainbow palettes.

---

## Primary Colors

Background:
Ultra-dark charcoal.

Surface:
Slightly lighter than the background.

Text:
Soft white.

Secondary text:
Muted gray.

Accent:
Professional cool blue.

Danger:
Deep red.

Success:
Emerald green.

Warning:
Amber.

---

## Usage Rules

Accent colors should highlight important actions only.

Never use more than one primary accent in the same section.

Maintain strong contrast between foreground and background.

Avoid pure black and pure white where possible.

---

## Gradients

Use gradients sparingly.

Gradients should support storytelling rather than decoration.

---

## Accessibility

Every color combination must maintain readable contrast.

Color must never be the only way to communicate important information.
