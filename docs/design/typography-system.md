# Typography System

## Philosophy

Typography is one of BLACKSITE's strongest visual elements.

It should communicate confidence and professionalism.

---

## Font Style

Modern sans-serif.

Readable.

Clean.

Minimal.

Avoid decorative fonts.

---

## Hierarchy

Headings should be bold and spacious.

Paragraphs should be comfortable to read.

Small text should remain readable.

---

## Rules

Never use more than two font families.

Maintain consistent spacing between headings and paragraphs.

Avoid centered text for long content.

Keep line lengths comfortable.

---

## Content Style

Write clearly.

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid unnecessary technical jargon.

Short paragraphs are preferred.

Every sentence should add value.

---

## Tone

Professional.

Confident.

Friendly.

Direct.

Never arrogant.

Never robotic.
